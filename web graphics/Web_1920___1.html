About Ali
Hi. I’m Ali. I design. I edit video. I make graphics move.

Since my Graphic Arts and Design degree I have gained almost 7 years of valuable commercial experience across a range of design, motion and editing disciplines, and enjoy bringing the skills I've developed in my diverse background to each new project.

I appreciate how good communication can help teams work together, and take pride in communicating clearly and effectively. I am always interested in the opinions of others, am keen to learn, and equally to share.

I love using my creative skills to catch the eyes and imaginations of others, striving to be as creative as I can whilst saying on-brand, meeting briefs, and working to tight deadlines.

Born and bred in Sana'a, I love to immerse myself in the amazing visual and performance art which surrounds me here, inspiring my own creations, with which I hope to spread the same joy and happiness.
